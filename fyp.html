Design and Fabrication of Organic Rankine Cycle Power Plant

Final Year Project

The project was funded by U.S.-Pakistan Centers for Advanced Studies in Energy (USPCAS-E), Islamabad and is currently operational there producing around a 1000 watts of electrical power output.

The main focus of the project was on employing solar irradiation as the heat source for driving the ORC, taking advantage of the compatibility between solar thermal collector technologies and the temperature needs of the cycle.

Sustainable Development Goals addressed by the project are:

  • SDG 7: Affordable and clean energy
  • SDG 12: Responsible consumption and production
  • SDG 13: Climate Action
